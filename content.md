## 5. UI Guidelines

- **Product-First:** Imagery should occupy at least 60% of the screen in key sections.
- **Implicit Intelligence:** Never use labels like "AI Recommended". Use "Picked for You" or "Recommended".
- **Clean Hierarchy:** Large whitespace, minimal borders, and strong typographic contrast.
- **No Technical Jargon:** Avoid mention of backend services, system modules, or infrastructure in customer-facing copy.
